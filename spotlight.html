QwikEyes In The News

QwikEyes has been featured in several media outlets over the years and here are a few we are most proud of. QwikEyes in the news Bryan attended the 2014 Clinton Global Initiative to represent QwikEyes as an invited participant. Prior to the conference 20 student ventures were invited to host an exhibit during the exhibition dinner where QwikEyes was presented to thousands of individuals including former president Clinton, his family, and media outlets. During the conference a live video recorded interview was conducted on Bryan regarding QwikEyes and what benefits the service has to its market. QwikEyes at CGIU In addition to the Clinton Global Initiative QwikEyes was competitively selected to receive $6,000 in seed funding, mentorship, and other resources to assist in the startup of the business. How did QwikEyes start In December of 2013 Qwik Eyes was adopted in to the Mesa Arizona Launch Point accelerator where the business was given office space, business resources such as a business phone number, mailing address, access to a conference room for meetings, and 50 hours of professional consulting to assist in different areas of the business development. QwikEyes adopted into Mesa LaunchPoint In July Qwik Eyes was invited to present the company and service during an Assistive Technology conference. The event is to discuss new assistive technology and services that are geared to help the blind and low vision. In attendance will be state of Arizona Vocational Rehabilitation counselors as well as blind individuals which are the direct market of Qwik Eyes.

Bryan is the CEO of QwikEyes which is a product and service under Augmented Humanity LLC
