    • About QwikEyes

    Qwik Eyes is a live video calling service that utilizes mobile technology for the purpose of providing sighted assistance to the blind. Qwik Eyes aims to augment the lives of the blind in educational, professional, personal, and social interaction. Utilizing the smart phone capabilities and built in camera, they can connect to Qwik Eyes in a matter of seconds for daily difficulties that the sighted take for granted. Callers will be greeted by a friendly and properly trained Qwik Eyes support specialist. The specialist will first inquire the reason for the call and than begin providing clear, accurate, detailed, and complete service. Qwik Eyes representatives know your time is valuable, so every caller will receive a professional and friendly service in a timely manner. It is the goal of Qwik Eyes to provide visual assistance to their customers during a time when organization fails, computer technology is inaccurate, and eyes become so crucial. In a time like this no one wants a long drawn out conversation that makes the task more time consuming than it already is. With Qwik Eyes our name is our philosophy, “Quick Eyes!”
